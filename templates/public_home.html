About US

At KAV Auditorium, we take pride in offering a warm, traditional, and beautifully designed space in the heart of Palakkad. Whether it's a wedding, celebration, cultural program, or convention, our hall provides an elegant and comfortable setting for every occasion. Located at a prime spot in Mundur, K.A.V Auditorium offers:

  • Seating capacity: 550
  • Floating capacity: 1000
  • Dining Hall: Located on the same floor for maximum convenience
  • Ample car parking
  • Water purifier facility
  • Generator backup to ensure your event continues smoothly, even during power cuts
We offer a range of customizable packages to suit your needs. Please contact us for more information.
